Common questions

Do my callers need WhatsApp?
Yes. The reply lands in WhatsApp, not as a text message. Most people already have it on their phone. If a caller does not use WhatsApp, they still see that you tried to reach them when you call back.
Do I need a new phone number?
No. Reception uses call forwarding on the number you already have. Your callers dial the same number they always have.
How long does setup take?
We handle the technical work. After you choose a plan, a short call is usually enough to connect forwarding and write your first messages. Most setups are done within a few days.
Does this work in my country?
It depends on your mobile carrier and whether call forwarding to our service is supported where you are. When you contact us, we check your number and country before you pay.
Can I change the message later?
Yes. Send us updated wording and we adjust your replies. You can also set different messages for business hours, evenings, and weekends.
